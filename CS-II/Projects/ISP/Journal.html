2-Jan-2023

I will discuss what positions are required for our ISP and duties the role has. The roles I am going to discuss are Software Development Engineer, Quality Assurance Engineer, Project Manager, Product Manager and Release Engineer.

Software Development Engineer

Software Development Engineer have a key role inside the team. This includes a collaborating with other team members to create underlying code. Also includes designing and documenting code. While also receiving feedback from managers and customers on what can be improved.

Quality Assurance Engineer

A quality assurance engineer will break the program. Well, try to break it I mean. By doing this they will find bugs and report them to get fixed. They will also create tests to prevent issues from happening and ensuring code stability.

Project Manager

A project manager is a leader for the developers. They need to communicate with the team to solve issues. They are also able to assist with deadline scheduling. They also need to coordinate events and meetings for the team insuring performance.

Product Manager

A product manager decides what gets "shipped" in the final product. They are responsible for telling the project manager what features they want. They are responsible for talking to the end customer about end features. While also creating estimates on when something will be done and update the customer.

Release Engineer

A release engineer is a final gate to the main push. There position requires them to proactively identify issues in the build process. They are required to understand a project to near entirety. Sometimes are required to troubleshoot issues with a program and can assist an QA engineer.
